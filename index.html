About

Hi! I am a graduate student at Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan and Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan. Previously, I graduated with the highest honor from Zhejiang University, where I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Yingcai Wu, Prof. Jake Zhao, and Prof. Chunhua Shen. I also spent a summer at Stanford working with Prof. Jiajun Wu's group.

My current research focuses on physics-based vision and computational imaging. During my undergraduate years, I worked on visual generative models as well as AI and VR for sports.
