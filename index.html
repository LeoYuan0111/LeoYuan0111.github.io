About

Hi, I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Statistics in Turing Class, Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. During my undergraduate, I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Yingcai Wu, Prof. Chunhua Shen, and Prof. Junbo Zhao. I also spent a lovely summer at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Jiajun Wu.

I am broadly interested in generative models and computer vision. Previously, I have authored two papers on visual and interactive computing for racket sports, stemming from my passion as a former semi-professional badminton player.

I'm actively looking for PhD position starting in Fall 2024. Please feel free to contact me if you have any leads :)
