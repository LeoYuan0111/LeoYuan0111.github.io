About

Hi! I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science in Turing Class, Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. During my undergraduate, I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Yingcai Wu, Prof. Jake Zhao and Prof. Chunhua Shen. I also spent a lovely summer at Stanford AI Lab working with Prof. Jiajun Wu's team.
