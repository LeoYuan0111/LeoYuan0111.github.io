About

Hi! I am a graduate student researcher at the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan and Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan. Previously, I graduated with the highest honor from Zhejiang University, where I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Yingcai Wu, Prof. Jake Zhao, and Prof. Chunhua Shen. I also spent a summer at Stanford AI Lab working with Prof. Jiajun Wu's group.

My research interest lies at the intersection of computer vision, graphics and computational imaging, with a focus on physics-based vision. During my undergraduate years, I worked on visual generative models and AI/VR systems for sports analytics.
