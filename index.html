About

Hi, I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science in Turing Class, Zhejiang University, jointly cultivated by Chu Kochen Honors College and College of Computer Science and Technology. Currently I am also a visiting research student at Stanford University.

I am interested in computer vision and machine learning. My research experience spans topics including 3D scene understanding, video understanding, generative models and data-efficient AI. I have also done engaging projects on VR for sports analytics and smart-eyewear-based HCI.

My ultimate research goal is to empower machines to learn the visual world through a 4D generative approach, and more importantly delve into the underpinning scientific principles of visual cognition, encompassing both machine learning and human perception.
