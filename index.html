About

Hi! I am a graduate student researcher at the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Srinivasa Narasimhan and Prof. Aswin Sankaranarayanan. Previously, I graduated with the highest honor from Zhejiang University, where I am grateful to be advised by Prof. Yingcai Wu, Prof. Jake Zhao, and Prof. Chunhua Shen. I also spent a summer at Stanford working with Prof. Jiajun Wu's group.

My research interest lies in physics-based vision, and more broadly, computer vision and computational imaging. Currently I study light transport and energy flow to address inverse problems in vision and graphics. During my undergraduate years, I worked on visual generative models and AI / VR for sports.
